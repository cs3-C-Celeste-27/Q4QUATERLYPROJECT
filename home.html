WHAT IS GENETICS?

Genetics is the scientific study of heredity and the variation of inherited characteristics. It encompasses the study of genes, which are units of heredity passed from parents to offspring, and how these genes influence traits such as physical appearance, behavior, and susceptibility to diseases.

WHAT IS GENETIC COUNSELING FOR?

Genetic counseling aims to empower individuals and families to make informed decisions about their genetic health, understand the implications of genetic conditions, and navigate the complexities of genetic testing and its results.

IS GENETIC COUNSELING THE SAME AS GENETIC TESTING?

WHAT CAN IT REVEAL ABOUT ME?

Genetics can offer valuable information about your health, ancestry, and biological relationships, empowering you to make informed decisions about your healthcare, lifestyle, and family planning.

WHAT ARE REASONS PEOPLE SEEK GENETIC COUNSELING?

Genetic counseling provides individuals and families with information, support, and guidance to make informed decisions about their genetic health, reproductive options, and medical care.
