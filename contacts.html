The NSGC is a professional organization for genetic counselors, offering information on finding a genetic counselor, understanding genetic conditions, and resources for patients and healthcare providers.
Genetics Home Reference provides consumer-friendly information about genetic conditions, genes, and chromosomes. It includes resources on genetic counseling and testing, as well as explanations of genetic concepts.
MedlinePlus offers reliable information on genetic conditions, genetic testing, and genetic counseling. It includes links to resources, articles, videos, and clinical trials related to genetics.
GGARD provides information on rare genetic disorders, including resources on genetic counseling, finding specialists, and support groups for patients and families affected by rare diseases.
The Genetic Support Foundation offers educational resources and support for individuals and families affected by genetic conditions. It includes information on genetic counseling, genetic testing, and advocacy initiatives.
